About

Alireza Moradi (also known as Adrian Morawdi) is a poet, literary critic, and independent researcher focused on narrative identity, character studies, and contemporary literary theory. His work explores the relationship between narrative structures and the formation of identity in literature.

He is particularly interested in theoretical approaches to character construction and has proposed the concept of Quantum Character Identity, examining how literary characters can exist across multiple interpretive states within narrative frameworks.
